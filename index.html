Who is James Borlagdan?

Hello, I am James, and I am a student at the FEU Institute of Technology taking up course, and this is my life outside the university belt and inside my favorite hobby, motorcycling

  • As a student
  • How it started
  • How It's Going

I have been interested in computers and have always had a passion for gaming and programming, ever since I was a little kid. I am currently in my third year of college, and I have consistently maintained this fascination. By pursuing this program, I am hoping to have the opportunity to participate in courses similar to this one, in which my abilities, imagination, and creativity will be evaluated, developed, and improved. This website fulfills all of the criteria I have set and satisfies all of my expectations for it. I would like to develop a website that depicts my life through the eyes of my favorite hobby—motorcycles.

During my first year in college back in 2019, I dealt with one of the most stressful aspects of being a Filipino college student: the daily commute. Hailing from Marikina, the daily routine involved waking up at 4 a.m. to prepare for my 7 a.m. classes. The stress and difficulty worsened when the LRT Line 2 broke down, pushing me to realize that I needed a more efficient mode of transportation. Thus, I made the decision to get a motorcycle, and from that point forward, every journey to school was not just a routine that I needed to do every day, but something more special. It wasn't just a means of getting from Marikina to FEU-Tech; it became a door to my new hobby, marking the start of my journey towards reaching new exciting destinations.

From there, I have explored destinations that I never thought of exploring. I tried to seek out new experiences, including partaking in an endurance challenge and riding on a bigger bike, which I discovered to be exceedingly fulfilling. Each location I've reached and the process of getting there have provided me with distinct pleasures and priceless knowledge about this hobby. After finishing these journeys, I have come to appreciate the essential value of putting safety as the foremost priority in order to gain a completely rewarding feeling from each expedition.
